==============================================================================================================
Loadibec for windows based on iRecovery. oibc on the way hopefully. Only for people who know what they are doing. Make sure you know how to get out of the recovery loop and how to unhide the oib menu.
==============================================================================================================
1. Run loadibec.bat
2. When prompted, type go, then enter
3. The openiboot image should now be loaded on your device
